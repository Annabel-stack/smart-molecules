Q: What is the functional group in alcohols?
A: The -OH group.

Q: What is the general formula of alkanes?
A: CnH2n+2

Q: Name a compound with a carboxylic acid group.
A: Ethanoic acid (CH3COOH)

Q: What is the difference between alkenes and alkynes?
A: Alkenes have double bonds; alkynes have triple bonds.
