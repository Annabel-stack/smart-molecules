Q: What is a homologous series?
A: A group of compounds with the same functional group and general formula

Q: What is the general formula for alkanes?
A: CnH2n+2

Q: How do members of a homologous series differ?
A: By a CH₂ unit

Q: Do members of a homologous series have similar chemical properties?
A: Yes, they react similarly

Q: What is the next member after ethane in the alkane series?
A: Propane (C₃H₈)
