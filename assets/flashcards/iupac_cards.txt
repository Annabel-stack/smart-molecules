Q: What does IUPAC stand for?
A: International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry

Q: What is the suffix for a compound with an OH group?
A: -ol (e.g., ethanol)

Q: What is the IUPAC name for CH₄?
A: Methane

Q: What is the IUPAC name for CH₃CH₂OH?
A: Ethanol

Q: What is the parent chain for CH₃CH₂CH₃?
A: Propane (3 carbons)
