Q: What are isomers?
A: Compounds with the same molecular formula but different structures

Q: What type of isomerism occurs in but-2-ene?
A: Geometric (cis-trans) isomerism

Q: Give an example of chain isomerism.
A: Butane and methylpropane

Q: What is functional group isomerism?
A: Isomers with different functional groups (e.g., alcohol vs. ether)

Q: What is optical isomerism?
A: Molecules that are mirror images and cannot be superimposed
